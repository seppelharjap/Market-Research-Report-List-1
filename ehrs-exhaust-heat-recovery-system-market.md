<p><p>The Exhaust Heat Recovery System (EHRS) market encompasses various technologies aimed at harnessing waste heat from exhaust gases to enhance efficiency. Exhaust gas heat recovery systems utilize heat exchangers to reclaim energy, while Rankine cycle systems convert waste heat into mechanical power through vaporization and condensation. Thermoelectric generators directly convert thermal energy into electricity, and Electric Turbo Compounding (ETC) integrates turbocharging to recover exhaust energy for additional power. Together, these technologies contribute to improved fuel efficiency and reduced emissions.</p></p>
