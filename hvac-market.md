<p><ul><li>Direct Expansion Systems</li><li>Central Air Conditioning Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HVAC market is primarily categorized into Direct Expansion (DX) Systems and Central Air Conditioning Systems. Direct Expansion Systems are self-contained units that cool air directly through refrigerant evaporation, ideal for smaller spaces and easy installation. In contrast, Central Air Conditioning Systems use a network of ducts to distribute cooled air throughout larger buildings, offering more comprehensive temperature control and efficiency but requiring more extensive installation. Both systems cater to diverse residential and commercial cooling needs, emphasizing energy efficiency and comfort.</p></p>
