<p><p>Patch management software operates in two primary market types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise solutions are installed locally on an organization’s servers, offering complete control over updates and security. This type is often favored by businesses with strict data compliance requirements. Conversely, cloud-based patch management is hosted on the vendor's servers, providing easy access, scalability, and automated updates, which can significantly reduce the IT burden. Organizations choose between these solutions based on their specific operational needs and resources.</p></p>
