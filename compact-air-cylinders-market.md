<p><p>The compact air cylinders market is categorized into two primary types: single acting and double acting cylinders. Single acting cylinders utilize air pressure to move the piston in one direction, relying on a spring to return it, making them ideal for applications needing limited movement. Double acting cylinders, on the other hand, employ pressurized air to move the piston in both directions, allowing for more versatile and powerful operation. Both types are essential for various automation and mechanical processes in industrial settings.</p></p>
