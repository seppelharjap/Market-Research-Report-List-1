In contrast, wireless visualizers utilize Wi-Fi or Bluetooth technology, offering greater flexibility and mobility, allowing users to connect without tangled cables. Each type serves different user needs, with wired options favored for permanence and wireless models preferred for convenience and ease of movement.</p></p>
