<p><ul><li>Class-A</li><li>Class-B</li><li>Class-AB</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The electronic tubes market is segmented into Class-A, Class-B, and Class-AB types based on their operating characteristics and efficiency. Class-A tubes offer high linearity and low distortion, making them suitable for high-fidelity applications, but are less efficient. Class-B tubes are more efficient, providing higher power output with increased distortion, ideal for amplifying signals. Class-AB tubes balance both efficiency and linearity, offering moderate distortion while allowing for better performance than Class-A, making them popular in various audio and communication devices.</p></p>
