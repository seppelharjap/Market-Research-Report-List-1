<p><p>The Attendance Management System market encompasses various types, primarily including proximity cards and biometrics. Proximity cards utilize radio frequency identification (RFID) technology, enabling quick attendance tracking by simply tapping or waving the card near a reader. In contrast, biometric systems authenticate attendance through unique physical traits such as fingerprints, facial recognition, or iris scans, ensuring higher accuracy and security. Together, these technologies enhance operational efficiency, reduce time fraud, and streamline workforce management across various sectors.</p></p>
