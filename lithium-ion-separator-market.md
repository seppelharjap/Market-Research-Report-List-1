<p><strong>The Lithium-ion Separator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monolayer Separator</li><li>Bilayer Separator</li><li>Trilayer Separator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The lithium-ion separator market consists of three primary types: monolayer, bilayer, and trilayer separators. Monolayer separators feature a single polymer layer, effectively preventing electrical short circuits while allowing ion transport. Bilayer separators incorporate two distinct layers to enhance thermal stability and improve overall performance. Trilayer separators combine three layers, often including a ceramic coating, for superior mechanical strength and reduced risks of thermal runaway. Each type caters to diverse applications, balancing safety, efficiency, and manufacturing costs.</p></p>
