<p><p>The automotive robotics market comprises several types, each serving distinct purposes. Articulated robots, featuring multiple joints, excel in tasks requiring flexibility and precision, such as welding and assembly. Cartesian robots offer linear motion along three axes, ideal for tasks like material handling and assembly. Cylindrical robots utilize a rotating joint and linear movement, suitable for applications needing vertical mobility. SCARA robots combine rotational and linear movements, perfect for assembly and pick-and-place activities. Other types may include delta robots and collaborative robots, enhancing automation efficiency.</p></p>
