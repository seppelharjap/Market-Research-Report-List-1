<p><p>The meteorological equipment market can be categorized into three types based on cloud conditions: Cold Cloud, Warm Cloud, and Mixed Phenomenon. Cold Cloud equipment is designed to measure and analyze precipitation from ice crystals and supercooled water droplets, while Warm Cloud equipment focuses on detecting liquid water droplets in warmer temperatures. Mixed Phenomenon equipment addresses both conditions, facilitating a comprehensive understanding of complex weather patterns. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing weather forecasting and climate research.</p></p>
