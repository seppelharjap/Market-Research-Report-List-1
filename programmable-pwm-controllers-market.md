<p><ul><li>Current Mode PWM Controllers</li><li>Voltage Mode PWM Controllers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The programmable PWM controllers market is primarily divided into two types: current mode PWM controllers and voltage mode PWM controllers. Current mode PWM controllers regulate output by monitoring inductor current, providing improved response to load variations and enhanced stability. In contrast, voltage mode PWM controllers adjust the output based on the desired voltage level, often featuring a simpler architecture but potentially slower transient response. Both types are integral in diverse applications such as power supplies, motor drives, and automotive systems.</p></p>
