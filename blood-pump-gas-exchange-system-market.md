<p><p>The Blood Pump Gas Exchange System encompasses two primary market types: Single Roller Pump Systems and Double Roller Pump Systems. Single Roller Pump Systems utilize a singular roller mechanism to efficiently transport blood while minimizing shear stress, catering to low-flow applications. Conversely, Double Roller Pump Systems employ two rollers for increased flow rates and enhanced performance, making them suitable for complex procedures requiring high precision. Both systems are integral in medical settings, ensuring effective oxygenation and circulation during various treatments.</p></p>
