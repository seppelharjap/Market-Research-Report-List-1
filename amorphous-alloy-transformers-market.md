<p><p>The amorphous alloy transformers market comprises primarily two types: oil-immersed and dry-type transformers. Oil-immersed amorphous alloy transformers utilize insulating oil for cooling and enhanced thermal management, making them suitable for high-capacity applications. In contrast, dry-type amorphous alloy transformers use air for cooling and are typically employed in indoor environments due to their safety and lower fire risk. Both types leverage amorphous metal technology, which improves energy efficiency and reduces losses in electrical distribution systems.</p></p>
