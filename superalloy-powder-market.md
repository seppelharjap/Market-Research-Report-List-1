<p><p>The superalloy powder market is categorized into three main types: iron-based, cobalt-based, and nickel-based superalloys. Iron-based superalloys offer good mechanical properties at elevated temperatures and are cost-effective, making them suitable for various applications. Cobalt-based superalloys provide excellent wear resistance and thermal stability, primarily utilized in high-temperature environments. Nickel-based superalloys exhibit outstanding performance in extreme conditions, widely used in aerospace and turbine industries due to their superior corrosion resistance and strength retention. Each type caters to specific industrial needs.</p></p>
