<p><p>Extraction balloons are specialized medical devices used in various procedures, primarily in endoscopic applications. The market includes triple-lumen balloons, which have three separate channels allowing for simultaneous functions such as extraction, aspiration, and inflation. Conversely, double-lumen balloons possess two channels, typically for inflation and suction. These designs cater to different procedural needs, with triple-lumen balloons offering greater versatility for complex procedures, while double-lumen balloons provide simpler, more streamlined solutions for less intricate tasks. Both types play critical roles in minimally invasive surgeries.</p></p>
