<p><p>Concentrated Photovoltaic (CPV) systems utilize lenses or mirrors to focus sunlight onto high-efficiency solar cells. The market is divided into two primary types: High Concentrated Photovoltaic (HCPV) and Low Concentrated Photovoltaic (LCPV). HCPV systems typically use higher concentrations of sunlight, often exceeding 1000 times, requiring precise tracking mechanisms. In contrast, LCPV systems concentrate sunlight at lower levels, around 2 to 10 times, and can be less complex and more adaptable to various terrains, making both types suitable for different applications.</p></p>
