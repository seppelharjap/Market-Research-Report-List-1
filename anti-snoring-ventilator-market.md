<p><p>The anti-snoring ventilator market is primarily categorized into two types: single-level ventilators and bi-level ventilators. Single-level ventilators deliver a constant airflow pressure to keep the airway open during sleep, effectively minimizing snoring. In contrast, bi-level ventilators provide varying pressure levels for inhalation and exhalation, allowing for a more comfortable breathing experience while addressing snoring issues. Both types cater to different user needs and preferences, enhancing sleep quality and reducing the health risks associated with snoring.</p></p>
