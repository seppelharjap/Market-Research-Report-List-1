<p><strong>The Injection Epoxy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Viscosity</li><li>Super Low Viscosity</li><li>Medium Viscosity</li><li>High Viscosity</li><li>Extra High Viscosity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The injection epoxy market is categorized into several viscosity types that cater to different applications. Low viscosity epoxies are ideal for deep penetration and bonding in tight spaces, while super low viscosity options excel in self-leveling applications. Medium viscosity products provide a balance of flow and strength, suitable for general repairs. High viscosity epoxies offer greater structural support for larger gaps, whereas extra high viscosity formulations are designed for demanding conditions, where minimal flow is required. Each type serves unique industrial needs.</p></p>
