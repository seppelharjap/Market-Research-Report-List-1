<p><p>The Automotive Full-SiC Power Module market features key configurations like Half-Bridge and Full-Bridge Modules. Half-Bridge Modules consist of two switches and are ideal for applications requiring moderate power handling, enabling efficient control of motors and converters. Full-Bridge Modules, on the other hand, integrate four switches to facilitate bidirectional power flow, making them suitable for high-power applications, such as electric vehicle drives and industrial machinery. Both types leverage silicon carbide technology for improved efficiency and thermal performance.</p></p>
