Rosin esters, made from natural rosin, provide good adhesion and are often used in pressure-sensitive adhesives. Terpene resins, sourced from pine trees, enhance tack while maintaining clarity and flexibility. Other tackifiers include various synthetic and bio-based options that cater to specific performance needs and environmental considerations.</p></p>
