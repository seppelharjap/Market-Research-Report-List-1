<p><p>The household washing machines market is primarily divided into two types: fully automatic and semi-automatic machines. Fully automatic washing machines handle the entire washing process with minimal user intervention, featuring advanced settings for washing, rinsing, and drying. They are convenient and efficient but typically come at a higher price. Semi-automatic machines require manual intervention between washing and rinsing cycles, offering more control over the process. They tend to be more affordable and are preferred in regions with unstable water supply or electricity issues.</p></p>
